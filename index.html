\[U_A(Y, Accept)=2X-Y\]

\[U_A(Y, Reject)=0\]

\[U_T(Y, Accept)=Y\]

\[U_T(Y, Reject)=X\]

\[E(X|X\le Y)=\frac{\int_{0}^{Y} x*f(x) dx }{F(Y)}\]

f(x)=-2x+2

\[=\int_{0}^{Y} x(-2x+2) dx= \]

\[F(Y)=Y(2-Y)\]
